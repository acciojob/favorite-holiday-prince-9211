Bali

Top 5 Attractions:

  1. Haleakalā National Park: Witness the sunrise from the summit of this dormant volcano.
  2. Road to Hana: Take a scenic drive along the winding road, passing waterfalls, rainforests, and stunning coastal views.
  3. Waianapanapa State Park: Explore the black sand beaches and lava caves.
  4. Snorkeling at Molokini Crater: Discover colorful marine life at this crescent-shaped volcanic crater.
  5. Old Lahaina Luau: Experience traditional Hawaiian culture with hula dancing, music, and delicious food.

Must-Try Foods:

  • Poke: Sample fresh raw fish marinated in delicious sauces.
  • Shave Ice: Cool off with this refreshing treat topped with fruity syrups.
  • Kalua Pig: Enjoy tender, slow-roasted pork cooked in an underground oven.
  • Lū‘au Kālua Chicken: Savor the flavors of Hawaiian-style roasted chicken.
  • Malasadas: Indulge in Portuguese-style fried doughnuts, often filled with creamy custard or fruity jams.
